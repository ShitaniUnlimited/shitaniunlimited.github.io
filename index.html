About Us

Founder - Victor Adetayo (2018-2022)

Development Lead - Adeboye Aregbesola (2021-Present)

Welcome to Shitani Unlimited, a game development group that started as a solo group back in 2018. We are proudly based in Nigeria and formerly based in Canada! We've previously been called, Spout, Peafox, Keplaa (Unlimited) and most recently Shinotani Studios! On August 30th, 2022, Hakata Interactive split from Shitani Unlimited along with founder Victor, Hakata Interactive is responsible for all games released before January 1st 2021, therefore if you are looking for Winathon or Winathon², please consult Hakata Interactive.
